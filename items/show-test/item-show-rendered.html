Peabody Hall Post-Its: "Let's Do Better Together"

Students covered the doors of Peabody Hall with post-its expressing a range of feelings about the Rolling Stone article. Most of the notes supported survivors, and many shared sentiments in favor of women's empowerment. Peabody Hall is home to the office of the Dean of Students and the Office of Admission.
